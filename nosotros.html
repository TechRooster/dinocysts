ABOUT ME


My name is Andree Alvarado, currently I reside in Lima, Perú. I am a graduate of Geological Engineering at the "Universidad Nacional de Ingeniería" in 2017, I have worked in the oil industry for two years and some months in the realization of geological, geotechnical and hydrogeological services. Currently I am doing my undergraduate thesis project at the "Universidad Peruana Cayetano Heredia", the project consists in the analysis of dinoflagellate cysts of the Paracas Bay.
