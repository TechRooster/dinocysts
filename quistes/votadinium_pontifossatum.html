VOTADINIUM PONTIFOSSATUM


Color: *Brownish

Surface: -

Shape: *Dorsoventrally compressed pentagonal ambitus. Length is greater than the width. Epicyst has straight or slightly convex sides with a pronounced apical horn. The antapical concavity is deep and U-shaped and the antapical horns are elongated.

Central body: *65 to 90 um. (length), 63 to 80 um. (width).

Process length: -

Process: *Ornamentation consist of small rod-like elements in circular depressions, covering the entire cyst including the operculum.

Archeopyle: *Saphopylic and pentagonal, though occasionally the entire apical part (the horn) can be missing.

Paratabulation: -

Septa: -

Cingulum: *Cingular area is sometimes devoid of ornamentation.

Sulcus: -

Distiguishing characteristics: -



DESCRIPTION:

Brownish cysts with a dorsoventrally compressed pentagonal ambitus. The length is greater than the width (ratio 1.18, n = 9). The epicyst has straight or slightly convex sides with a pronounced apical horn. The antapical concavity is deep and Ushaped and the antapical horns are elongated. The ornamentation consists of small rod-like elements in circular depressions, covering the entire cyst including the operculum. Archeopyle saphopylic and pentagonal, though occasionally the entire apical part (the horn) can be missing. The cingular area is sometimes devoid of ornamentation. Based on Gurdebeke et al. (2019a). (Extracted from Mertens et al., 2019).



STRATIGRAPHIC RANGE:

Holocene, surface and trap sediments off Vancouver Island in Canada, as well as modern sediments from Omura Bay in Japan, Fangchenggang Port (South China Sea), and coastal sediment from Europe (U.K. and northern Germany) (Gurdebeke et al., 2019a). (Extracted from Mertens et al., 2019).
