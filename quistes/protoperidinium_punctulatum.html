PROTOPERIDINIUM PUNCTULATUM


Color: *Dark brown.

Surface: -

Shape: *Spherical.

Central body: *52 to 65 um. (diameter).

Archeopyle dimensions: **20 to 30 um. (diameter).

Process length: -

Process: -

Archeopyle: *Saphopylic, 2a intercalary. Hexagonal, elongate equatorially with a length:breadth ratio around 0.6, and asymmetrical.

Paratabulation: -

Septa: -

Cingulum: -

Sulcus: -

Distiguishing characteristics: -



DESCRIPTION:

Large dark brown spherical cysts with a relatively large, saphopylic 2a intercalary archeopyle. The archeopyle is hexagonal, elongate equatorially with a length:breadth ratio around 0.6, and asymmetrical. Based on Wall and Dale (1968). (Extracted from Mertens et al., 2019).



ECOLOGY:

Modern sediments from Woods Hole (Wall and Dale, 1968), British Columbia, Canada (Dobell, 1978), Denmark (Ellegaard et al., 1994) and Australia (Sonneman and Hill, 1997). (Extracted from Mertens et al., 2019).
