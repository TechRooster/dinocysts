CLASIFICATION


The classification of dinoflagellates described in this page is made considering the morphological aspect of the dinoflagellates that can be recognized through the use of the standard transmitted light microscope.


The main guide used on this page was prepared by Karin Zonneveld and Vera Pospelova.


Zonneveld, K.A.F. and Pospelova V. (2015). A determination key for modern dinoflagellate cysts. Palynology 39 (3), 387-409


Each one of the images has its respective reference, do not forget to cite correctly the picture you want to extract.


One of the weaknesses in the information I had access to was the scarcity of images, and I intend for this page to have as many images as possible, in order to achieve a much faster identification.
