WELCOME!!


Visitor, have a great daey. My name is Andree Alvarado and I have created this page as a support resource for the preparation of my research thesis.


Identification of dinoflagellate cysts is a very important part of this research by using transmitted light techniques on polarization microscope. Therefore, this website will be oriented to develop a practical way of cyst identification and will be based on ther guides elaborated by experts in this field.


In addition to this, other studies, explanations and summaries will be included in order to have a solid theoretical foundation, which in turn allows to show in a simple way the nature of dinoflagellates and other additional properties.


I hope you find this resource as useful as I do.
