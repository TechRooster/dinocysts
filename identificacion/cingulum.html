CINGULUM


The Latin word cingulum merely means girdle or zone and can be applied equally readily to the region of the cyst between the anterior and posterior sections as it can to the corresponding region of the theca.



Sarjeant, W. A. (1982). Dinoflagellate cyst terminology: a discussion and proposals. Canadian journal of botany, 60(6), 922-945. pp. 942.
